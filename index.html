"Boost Your Voice"

During the presidential election, Boost launched a nationwide initiative for equal voting access.
We teamed up with our Boost stores acoss the country as polling places to help Americans have an equal voice.
The campaign leveraged Boost's brand positioning, awareness and retail locations to create a real change.

Tactics

Starting in Boost markets with low voter turnout, we wanted to launch a nationwide social and localized grassroots program to address the issues that kept people from voting. More importantly, we wanted to turn Boost Mobile stores into official polling places.

1. DEvelop and Launch Film

2. develop microsite

3. Launch change.org petition

4. Experiential Events

Results

More than 15+ awards, 23% increase in votes cast, outpacing the national average, 766 Million earned media impressions and Boost store sales grew 29% YOY in stores where activated.
